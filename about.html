Who We Are

Leading Provider of Integrated Solutions

CALI Environment & Support Services is a family-founded business established in 2010, providing comprehensive cleaning, facilities management, and care support services across London and the surrounding areas. What began as a small cleaning operation has grown into a trusted partner for over 300 clients, supported by approximately 200 dedicated staff members and a professional management team.

Our Mission

Delivering Excellence, Every Time

Transforming Spaces, Enriching Lives

Our mission is to deliver and maintain the highest standards of professional service across all sectors we serve. We promote environments of cleanliness, safety, and comfort while ensuring complete peace of mind for property owners, managing agents, landlords, educational institutions, healthcare facilities, and corporate clients.

At CALI, we bring together cleaning, care, and facilities management services that elevate quality of life. Our objective is to achieve a competitive edge through exceptional service delivery, successfully matching our entrepreneurial spirit with the evolving needs of our diverse client base.
