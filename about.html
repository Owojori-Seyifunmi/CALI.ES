Who We Are

Leading Provider of Integrated Solutions

CALI Environment & Support Services is a family-founded business established in 2010, providing comprehensive cleaning and facilities management services across London and the surrounding areas. What began as a small cleaning operation has grown into a trusted partner for over 300 clients, supported by approximately 200 dedicated staff members and a professional management team.
