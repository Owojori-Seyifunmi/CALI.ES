Common Questions

Pricing FAQs

Everything you need to know about our pricing and plans

Can I change my plan later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. If you upgrade mid-cycle, we'll prorate the difference.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), ACH bank transfers, and wire transfers for enterprise accounts. All payments are processed securely.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees! We believe in straightforward pricing. The price you see is the price you pay. We'll schedule an initial consultation to understand your needs and start service right away.

Do you offer discounts for long-term contracts?

Yes! Annual plans save 20% compared to monthly billing. We also offer special pricing for multi-year contracts and multi-location agreements. Contact our sales team for custom quotes.

What if I need service outside my plan's coverage?

We offer flexible add-on services that can be purchased individually or added to your monthly plan. You can also book one-time deep cleaning or special services as needed.

What's included in the satisfaction guarantee?

If you're not completely satisfied with our service, we'll return within 24 hours to address any concerns at no additional cost. If you're still not happy, we'll refund that service visit.

Can I cancel my plan anytime?

Monthly plans can be cancelled with 30 days notice. Annual plans have a minimum 12-month commitment but can be cancelled with prorated refund after 6 months for a valid business reason.

Do prices include cleaning supplies?

Yes! All plans include professional-grade cleaning supplies and equipment. We bring everything needed for the services included in your plan. Add-on services may have additional supply costs.

How do I get started with Enterprise pricing?

Contact our sales team for a free consultation. We'll assess your facility, understand your needs, and provide a detailed custom quote within 48 hours. Enterprise plans typically start at $2,000/month.

Are there any hidden fees?

Absolutely not. We believe in transparent pricing. The monthly price includes all standard services, supplies, and support listed in your plan. Any additional services are clearly quoted before work begins.
